Web Application for University Housing: UX Process

Problem Description & Goal

Currently, my university still use a manual housing selection process. Students need to physically show up at a room during their assigned time, wait in line, and pick their housing after wait in line for at least 15 minutes. This current process is time-consuming, and induce students' stress. The goal for this project is to understand the housing selection procedure; design and implement a solution to increase the efficiency of this procedure within 10 weeks.

Some of the questions that guided me through the process:
      -Who are the users?
      -How to reduce users stress?
      -How will users use this?
      -Will users use this system?
